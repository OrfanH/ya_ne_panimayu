# VISION.md — Один Семестр

## What this is
A browser-based pixel art language RPG. You play a foreign student
who just arrived in a small Russian town for a semester abroad.
You explore the town freely, meet people, get missions, and gradually
learn to navigate Russian daily life. The learning is invisible —
it happens through living in the game world, not through studying.

## The feeling
Stardew Valley meets a semester abroad. Warm pixel art town.
NPCs with real personalities and memory. Missions that feel like
life tasks, not exercises. Progress that feels like friendships
built and a town slowly understood.

## The world is the curriculum
The map unlock sequence IS the A1-to-A2 learning path.
Apartment (A1 pure) → Park (A1 consolidating) →
Cafe (A1+) → Market (A1+) → Station (A2 emerging) →
Police station (A2).
Every unlock has a story reason. The player feels invited,
not levelled up.

## The story premise
You are a student. You know nobody. You need to build a life here
for one semester. That is all the premise you need. The rest
unfolds through the people you meet.

NOTE: STORY.md and WORLD.md do not exist yet. They will be generated
in a dedicated session. No NPC names, mission details, or story beats
should be invented by agents until those files exist and are approved.

## What this is NOT
- Not a flashcard app with a pixel art skin
- Not Duolingo with a map
- Not a lesson viewer with a character sprite
- Not a game where the Russian is optional or skippable
- Not a game that rewards time spent over accuracy

## Definition of done for v1
A player can open the app, walk freely around the town, enter the
apartment building, meet their neighbour, complete 3 missions across
the first chapter, unlock the park, meet someone there, and genuinely
feel like they are living in Russia — while having learned 20-30
Russian words and phrases without noticing they were studying.
