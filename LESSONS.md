# Curriculum Overview

10 units, ~40 lessons, A1 to solid A2.

## Block 1 — Foundation

### Unit 1: Cyrillic Alphabet (7 lessons)
1-1. Familiar letters (А К М О С Т)
1-2. False friends (В Е Н Р У Х)
1-3. New shapes (Б Г Д З И Л)
1-4. More new shapes (П Ф Э Ц Ч Ш Щ)
1-5. Soft and hard signs (Ъ Ь)
1-6. Vowel pairs (Я Ё Ю)
1-7. Reading practice + stress rules

### Unit 2: First Words & Survival Phrases (5 lessons)
2-1. Greetings
2-2. Introductions
2-3. Essential questions
2-4. Numbers 1-20
2-5. At a cafe

## Block 2 — Core Grammar

### Unit 3: Noun Gender & Plurality (3 lessons)
3-1. Masculine, feminine, neuter
3-2. Plural nouns
3-3. Irregular plurals

### Unit 4: Present Tense Verbs (4 lessons)
4-1. First conjugation (-ать/-ять)
4-2. Second conjugation (-ить)
4-3. Irregular verbs
4-4. Negation

### Unit 5: The Case System (7 lessons)
5-1. Nominative
5-2. Accusative
5-3. Genitive
5-4. Dative
5-5. Instrumental
5-6. Prepositional
5-7. Cases in action (combined practice)

### Unit 6: Adjectives (3 lessons)
6-1. Agreement (gender + number)
6-2. Endings by case
6-3. Common adjectives

## Block 3 — Everyday Language

### Unit 7: Family, People, Descriptions (3 lessons)
7-1. Family members
7-2. Describing people
7-3. Possessives

### Unit 8: Time, Days, Dates, Numbers (4 lessons)
8-1. Days and months
8-2. Telling time
8-3. Numbers 20-1000
8-4. Dates and years

### Unit 9: Verb Aspects (3 lessons)
9-1. Imperfective
9-2. Perfective
9-3. Aspect pairs in context

### Unit 10: Everyday Situations (4 lessons)
10-1. At the shop
10-2. Getting around
10-3. At the doctor
10-4. Making plans
