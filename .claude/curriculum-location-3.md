## Location 3: Cafe (Кафе)

**Words introduced:**
- я хочу · I want · verb phrase
- дайте мне · give me · imperative phrase
- можно · may I / is it possible · modal particle
- можно мне · may I have · polite request phrase
- не могли бы вы · could you please · formal request phrase
- кофе, чай, вода, сок · coffee, tea, water, juice · noun
- хлеб, суп, салат · bread, soup, salad · noun
- сколько стоит · how much does it cost · phrase
- десять–сто (10–100) · ten through one hundred · numeral
- рубль / рублей · rouble / roubles · noun
- счёт · the bill · noun

**Words reappearing from Location 2 in new context:**
- большой / маленький — ordering a large or small coffee ("большой кофе, пожалуйста")
- красивый — Boris Gennadyevich describes a view from the window; player has the word
- как — "Как вас зовут?" from Lena; player recognizes the question word
- directions (здесь/там) — locating a free table ("Там есть место?")
- numbers 1–10 — reused as the base layer for prices in the 10–100 range

**Structures introduced:**
- Ordering food and drink using three request levels: я хочу / можно мне / дайте мне
- Asking the price and understanding a number as an answer
- Paying and receiving change (numbers 10–100 in a real transaction)
- Unlabelled accusative: the object of "I want" naturally takes accusative form

**Unlock gate — what the player must demonstrate:**
Order at least one item using a complete request phrase, ask the price, and state the correct number when paying.
