## Location 4: Market (Рынок)

**Words introduced:**
- картошка, морковь, яблоко, помидор · potato, carrot, apple, tomato · noun
- килограмм / кило, полкило · kilogram, half a kilo · noun/phrase
- свежий · fresh · adjective
- спелый · ripe · adjective
- хороший · good (quality) · adjective
- я возьму это / я возьму вот это · I'll take this / I'll take that · phrase
- мне не нужно · I don't need / want · phrase
- сдача · change (money) · noun
- дорого / дёшево · expensive / cheap · adverb

**Words reappearing from Location 3 in new context:**
- numbers 10–100 — prices at market stalls (same number range, new transactional context: weight × price)
- можно — "Можно посмотреть?" (Can I look?) — same particle, new request
- рубль / рублей — paying Fatima; change calculation
- сколько стоит — asking stall prices; player now initiates it without prompting
- хлеб — Misha's stall has bread; word recurs in a supply rather than ordering context
- большой / маленький — choosing a larger or smaller quantity

**Structures introduced:**
- Naming a quantity and a product together: "полкило картошки"
- Accepting and declining a purchase politely
- Comparing two items using adjectives (этот свежее — this one is fresher)
- Paying and checking change from a market stall

**Unlock gate — what the player must demonstrate:**
Complete a full market transaction — name a product, state a quantity, ask the price, pay, and use мне не нужно to decline at least one unwanted item.
