  A: "Я из [place]." (I am from [place].)

### Variation 4: Tea and Politeness Drill

GALINA: Чай? (Tea?) — *she leaves the question short, watching how you answer, not just whether you answer.*

PLAYER choices:
  A: "Да, пожалуйста." (Yes, please.)
  B: "Нет, спасибо." (No, thank you.)
  C: "Да." or "Нет." (Yes. or No. — alone, without the polite word.)

GALINA response to A: — *she steps back from the door, satisfied* — Хорошо. — *while pouring, she mentions it the way someone mentions a weather fact* — После десяти — тишина. (After ten o'clock — silence.) Такие правила. (Those are the rules.) Десять часов. (Ten o'clock.) Запомните. (Remember that.)
GALINA response to B: — *she accepts this without complaint, just nods* — Хорошо. Спасибо — правильно. (Good. "Thank you" — correct.) — *a small approving gesture.*
GALINA response to C: — *her eyebrow lifts, only slightly. She models without scolding* — Да, пожалуйста. (Yes, please.) Нет, спасибо. (No, thank you.) — *she waits* — Ещё раз. (Once more.)

### Variation 7: Spare Key Scene

GALINA: Вот ключ. (Here is the key.) — *she holds it out but does not release it yet* — Это один ключ. Один. (This is one key. One.) Если потеряете — нет нового ключа. (If you lose it — there is no new key.) Понятно? (Understood?)

PLAYER choices:
  A: "Спасибо. Да, я понимаю." (Thank you. Yes, I understand.)
  B: "Спасибо." (Thank you. — alone, without the confirmation.)
  C: "Нет." (No.)

GALINA response to A: — *she releases the key with a nod. Business concluded.* — Хорошо. — *almost to herself* — Один ключ. Дверь — это дверь, не проблема. (Good. One key. The door is the door, not a problem.)
GALINA response to B: — *she does not let go yet. Gently.* — Да? Вы понимаете? (Yes? You understand?) — *she waits for the да. She is patient but she is waiting.*
