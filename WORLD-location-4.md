# WORLD — Location 4: Market (Рынок)

## Location 4: Market (Рынок)

**The place:**
An open-air market in a covered arcade — a long row of stalls under a metal roof that keeps out most of the rain. Produce stalls, household goods, a few stalls with clothing and shoes. Fatima's stall is roughly in the middle on the left. Misha's is further down on the right.

Crowded on weekday mornings, emptying by afternoon. The smells change by section — vegetables, spices, something mechanical from the far end.

**The feel:**
The most socially complex location so far. Multiple vendors, a crowd, things happening in the background. The player has to navigate to specific stalls, deal with multiple characters, make choices. It is a contained social challenge.

**8+ distinct visit reasons across the full game:**

1. First visit — finding Fatima's stall; first transaction with real stakes (she won't sell until you ask properly)
2. Shopping missions: buy a list, compare prices, fetch from a specific stall
3. Misha encounters: find the lost item, the translation mission, inventory count
4. Reinforcement: vocabulary from cafe and apartment reappears here in new contexts (numbers, colors, adjectives)
5. Cross-NPC evidence: Fatima mentions Galina Ivanovna, Artyom; the town's web tightens
6. Afternoon/rainy-day variants: the market changes texture at different times; near-close visits feel different
7. Late-game shopping: player can now conduct full transactions without prompting; this is tracked silently
8. Final chapter provision: Fatima sends player off with something before the station visit; a quiet story beat
9. Background observation: NPCs the player hasn't met are visible at other stalls; the world feels bigger than what's interactive

**NPC cross-references active here:**
- Fatima references Galina Ivanovna, Artyom, Lena
- Misha references Artyom, Fatima

**Vocabulary anchored here:** Produce and household item names, quantities, colors, quality adjectives, paying and receiving change, declining politely
